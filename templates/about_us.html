ABOUT US

Welcome to Waste Whirl!

Our endeavour is to connect you with the ragpickers around and take a step forward towards a better and healthy future. We work to provide you with the best services at your fingertips.

Our Services

Dual Login ID System

We offer a secure and user-friendly login system for both the clients and ragpickers.

Appointment Booking

You can book appointments according to the availability of the ragpickers and your favourable timings.

Service at your Doorstep

The rag is collected from your doorstep by provision of convenient address setup system.

Payment Gateway

The money according to the disposed off rag by you is credited to your account within 2-3 working days.

Tip & Salary System

Each ragpicker associated with us gets a fixed salary by the end of the month. Your satisfaction for the service can be expressed as a tip for the ragpickers by you. It motivates them to do their work more efficiently.

Review System

Authentic ratings for every ragpicker shared by customers are visible which assist you to choose better services.
